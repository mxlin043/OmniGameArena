You control Player 1, the blue character in a top-down 3D PvP arena. Race the red Player 2 to complete weapon orders and score more points before the timer runs out.

## What you see on screen
- The screen shows total score, Game Time countdown, and Current orders list.
- World labels identify weapons or stations. The green label above your character shows what you are carrying.
- Interaction prompts ("PickUp", "Upgrade", "Submit", "Discard") appear when you are close enough.

## Rules
- Weapons are order items, not attack tools. Types: Rifle, Pistol, Axe, Knife.
- Both players share the same active orders. A matching submission at the Order Counter scores points and removes that order.
- Use your own weapons, WorkBench, and Bin.
- Carry at most one weapon. For normal orders, pick up the matching weapon and submit it. For High-grade orders, upgrade it first until the green label says "High-grade <weapon>".
- If carrying the wrong weapon, discard it at your Bin.
- The episode ends when Game Time reaches zero.
