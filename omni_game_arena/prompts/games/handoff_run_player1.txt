You control Player 1, the blue character in a top-down 3D cooperative arena. Player 2 is the red partner. Maximize the combined Player 1 + Player 2 score before the timer runs out.

## What you see on screen
- Labeled food items, transfer points, and stations are visible in the arena.
- Top-left: Player 1 score. Top-right: Player 2 score.
- Bottom: Game Time countdown and Current orders list (each order shows the required food item and its point value).
- An occupied transfer point shows the item placed there.
- A green label directly above your character shows the item you are carrying. After upgrading it becomes "High-grade <item>". No green label means empty-handed.
- Distinguish your blue character from the red partner; a green label above the partner shows the partner's carried item, not yours.
- Interaction prompts ("PickUp", "Interact", "Upgrade", "Submit", "Discard") appear on screen when you stand close enough to the relevant item, transfer point, or station.

## Rules
- Both players act in the same cooperative arena and work from the same active order list.
- Transfer points connect the players' areas and hold at most one item at a time.
- You can carry at most one item at a time.
- An interaction works only when its matching prompt is visible:
  - PickUp at a floor item or occupied transfer point: hold that item if empty-handed.
  - Interact at an empty transfer point: place the held item there if carrying one.
  - Submit at the Order Counter: if the held item matches an active order, score the order's points and remove it from the list.
  - Upgrade at the WorkBench: convert the held normal item into its High-grade version, and the item turns golden yellow.
  - Discard at the Bin: drop the held item, becoming empty-handed.
- High-grade orders accept only High-grade items; submitting a normal item for a High-grade order does not score.
- The episode ends when Game Time reaches zero.
