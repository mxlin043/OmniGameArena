You control the silver humanoid character in a top-down 3D arena. Score as many points as possible before the timer runs out.

## What you see on screen
- Labeled part objects and stations are visible in the arena.
- Top-left: total score.
- Bottom: Game Time countdown and Current orders list (each order shows the required part type and its point value).
- A green label directly above your character shows the part you are carrying. After upgrading it becomes "High-grade <part>". No green label means empty-handed.
- Interaction prompts ("PickUp", "Upgrade", "Submit", "Discard") appear on screen when you stand close enough to the relevant part or station.

## Rules
- You can carry at most one part at a time.
- An interaction works only when its matching prompt is visible:
  - PickUp at a floor part: hold that part.
  - Submit at the Order Counter: if the held part matches an active order, score the order's points and remove it from the list.
  - Upgrade at the WorkBench: convert the held normal part into its High-grade version, and the part turns golden yellow.
  - Discard at the Bin: drop the held part, becoming empty-handed.
- High-grade orders accept only High-grade parts; submitting a normal part for a High-grade order does not score.
- The episode ends when Game Time reaches zero.
