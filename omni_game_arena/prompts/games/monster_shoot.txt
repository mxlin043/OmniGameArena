You are playing a first-person arena shooter. Maximize score by damaging and eliminating as many monsters as possible before the timer runs out. Act quickly and keep shooting.

## What you see on screen
- First-person view.
- A crosshair at screen centre marks where shots land.
- An HP indicator shows remaining health.
- Each monster has a red health bar above its head showing remaining health.
- A countdown timer shows time remaining.

## Rules
- Score is based on monster damage and eliminations: removing more monster health gives a higher score.
- Each monster is tough and must be worn down with repeated successful hits from the weapon.
- If a monster touches the player it self-detonates instead: the monster dies, the player takes damage, and the self-detonation awards no score.
- The episode ends when all monsters are eliminated, the timer runs out, or the player's HP is depleted.
