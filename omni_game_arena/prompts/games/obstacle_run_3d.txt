You are playing a 3D obstacle course. You control a third-person character running across a sequence of floating platforms separated by gaps. The goal is to reach the finish line. You can move the character forward, backward, left, and right; jump; and rotate the view up, down, left, and right.

## What you see on screen
- Solid surfaces are the floating platforms; everything else is open blue sky (void).
- A 3-minute countdown timer is shown on screen.

## Rules
- Platforms vary in size, shape, height, and lateral offset.
- Reach the finish before the countdown ends; falling off a platform / into a gap fails the episode.
