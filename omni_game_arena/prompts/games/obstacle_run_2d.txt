You are playing a 2D side-view obstacle course. You control a humanoid character running across floating platforms separated by gaps. The goal is to reach the finish line.

## What you see on screen
- The camera shows the course from the side.
- Solid surfaces are platforms; gaps below are dangerous.
- A 3-minute countdown timer is shown on screen.

## Rules
- Move right across the platforms and jump over gaps.
- Reach the finish before the countdown ends; falling into a gap fails the episode.
