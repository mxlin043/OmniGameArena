You control Player 2, the red-and-white fighter, in a third-person 1v1 melee duel on a square platform floating high in the sky. Player 1 is the black armored opponent. Defeat Player 1 before they defeat you.

## What you see on screen
- Third-person view of your fighter and the floating platform arena.
- The sky / empty space outside the platform is void; falling into it is fatal.
- Your HP is shown on the right health bar, and the opponent's HP is shown on the left health bar.
- Player 1 appears as a dark black-armored fighter.

## Rules
- A successful melee hit deals 10 HP damage to the opponent.
- Melee attacks only matter when the opponent is close and in front of your fighter.
- Falling off the platform deals 100 HP damage and is an immediate defeat.
- The episode ends when either player reaches 0 HP or falls off the platform.
