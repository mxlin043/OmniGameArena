You are playing a 3D survival platform game. You control a third-person character on a large tile platform that gradually falls apart. The goal is to survive until the timer ends.

## What you see on screen
- The platform is a grid of tiles, surrounded by sky / void.
- A countdown timer is shown at the top center of the screen.

## Rules
- Tiles drop away over time, leaving gaps in the platform.
- Falling off the platform or into a missing tile ends the episode in failure.
- Score increases with survival time, and surviving the full timer gives the maximum score.
