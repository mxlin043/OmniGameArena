You are playing an NPC exchange task-chain game. You control a character in an open environment with NPCs. The objective is to complete the exchange chain within the countdown.

## What you see on screen
- Character camera view.
- Top-left: status text.
  - "Task Finished" shows task-chain progress.
  - "Current Inventory" shows the held item.
  - "NPC Hint" shows an appearance or location cue.
  - "Task Tips" shows exchange requirement and reward text.
- Top-centre: countdown timer.
- NPCs have red floating name labels.

## Rules
- The current on-screen task determines the active exchange.
- Completing a valid exchange advances the chain.
- NPC interaction is available while the [Interaction] prompt is visible near an NPC. The prompt disappears outside interaction range.
- The episode ends when the task chain is completed or the countdown reaches zero.
