You control Player 2, the red-side team, in a two-player BaseAssault crystal assault match. Player 1 is the blue-side opponent. The view is first-person through a crosshair. Destroy the opposing crystal before Player 1 destroys yours.

## What you see on screen
- First-person crosshair view of the arena.
- Your crystal sits inside the red-side base; the opposing crystal sits inside the blue-side base.
- Iron gate shields stand near each team's spawn area, separate from the crystals. They block baseball projectiles and the line of sight to the crystal behind them.
- Your crystal HP and your player HP are shown on the right health bars; the opposing crystal HP and the opposing player HP are shown on the left health bars.
- A countdown timer at top center.

## Rules
- Both crystals start with 100 HP. Both players start with 100 HP.
- LMB fires one baseball projectile in the crosshair direction.
- A baseball hit deals 50 HP to a player and 10 HP to a crystal. Iron gates absorb shots and take no damage.
- A defeated player respawns at full HP behind their own iron gate shield; respawns are unlimited.
- The episode ends when either crystal reaches 0 HP or when the timer reaches zero.
